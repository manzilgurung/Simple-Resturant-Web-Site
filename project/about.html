History of our restaurant

KK-Restaurant began on March, 2005 in the city of Jorethang. Focusing on the goals of “Quality, Healthy, and Tradition” the company is family operation making each dish in our own kitchens from scratch.KK-Restaurant has three generations of family member working together to fulfill the idea “Honor the Customer, Quality Comes First”. Through hard work and dedication,KK-Restaurant began building its brand around the idea of delicious and authentic traditional Western food.
